# In-Game-MouseRecorderForMacro
available for ANY FPSgame, generate your recoil trace and use it for Macro within 10s


This is how you gonna use this:

STEP1: open the exe file,input your RPM(900 800 600....) and Magazinecapacity(30, 35, 50...)
<img width="971" height="394" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/8c74cd6d-14e5-4577-b209-5ee3e9ac8ee1" />

STEP2: back to your game, to your solo training room or whatever, hold right button to aim down sight then open fire, control the recoil as possible
<img width="978" height="340" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/f2113579-7535-447c-b15d-9829931ff898" />

<img width="1584" height="888" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/c620225c-324a-4a62-be84-0bddd76bcd47" />
STEP3: back to the exe terminal you just opened,you can see the Xmove=... Ymove=... Tmove=..., thats how your mouse just moved, X axis Y axis and T axis(frequency)
<img width="969" height="344" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/a95006ad-3586-4055-aa4a-5507ff51cf4a" />

STEP4: copy XmoveYmoveTmove then go to the AntiRecoilMacroLua(Ghub).txt, replace the original XYTmove part
<img width="977" height="664" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/64126c0e-879b-4a83-a473-2709b853da91" />
STEP5: copy the entire txt file, paste into your Ghub LuaScript, click button6 to activate this macro(if you dont know which one is button6,open the Ghub Lua console,press every button of your mouse,it will tell you the number of the button you just pressed)
STEP6: you re ready to go,back to your game and enjoy the 0-recoil
